IPL DATA ANALYSIS - KEY INSIGHTS

1. Virat Kohli emerged as one of the highest run scorers in IPL history, demonstrating long-term consistency.

2. Players with high strike rates are not always the highest run scorers, indicating a trade-off between aggression and consistency.

3. Mumbai Indians and Chennai Super Kings maintained strong win percentages compared to most teams.

4. Orange Cap winners consistently scored significantly more runs than the league average in their respective seasons.

5. Virat Kohli's performance peaked during certain seasons, showing noticeable variation in run production across years.

6. Team success is influenced by both batting strength and consistency across seasons rather than individual player performance alone.

7. Season-wise analysis shows that modern IPL seasons tend to feature higher scoring performances compared to earlier years.
